Emergency thoracotomy due to cardiac arrest after penetrating chest trauma

A thoracotomy is a surgical procedure in which a cut is made between the ribs to see and reach the lungs or other organs in the chest or thorax. You’re looking at the heart and lung from below, as if you were standing at the patient’s feet while they’re lying supine in bed. Here, you’re seeing the lingula of the left lung and the apex of the heart.
